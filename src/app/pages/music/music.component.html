MUSIC

Music has always been a passion of mine. I have trained myself in various instruments and studied music since the age of 6. My background for music is a mixture of classical and modern instruments that I have played in various bands and also orchestras during my time in the Music School.


I compose music using mainly FL Studio, but I also have some experience with Ableton Live and Pro Tools. My genres of music vary greatly and my versatility is one of my greatest assets. I have also produced music for others such as Bohemian Ronin and Selina Hakala.


I also have experience on creating soundtracks for games and live mixing for various events.
