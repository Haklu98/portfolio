MUSIC

My journey into music started in 2001, when I let my parents know that I want to play the violin. After that, I have stumbled across other instruments and music production, as well as composing music. I'm most proficient with the guitar, piano and violin, but I also have experience with other instruments.


I started composing my own music already back in 2006 and I took on producing music in 2012. In 2018 I really started to focus on music production and I released my first own single that I produced myself. After that I have done various other projects and commissions for other artists too.


I also have experience on creating soundtracks for games and live mixing for various events. These activities started in 2024 and I have been working on them and gathering as much experience as possible.
