Elli-Ticorporate

Description

Elli was our team's FF Ticorporate project during the 2024 autumn. This project was a commission given to us by Elisa Oyj as a part of their collaboration program with Jamk. Our goal was to create a collaboration tool between Jamk and Elisa Oyj to provide better communication with transparency and efficiency.


My job was mainly on the frontend side of the project. I spent copious amounts of time on the different calendar and task management features, as well as the filtering and confirmation modules. Working with Angular and Tailwind, I was able to create a visually appealing and user-friendly interfac that followed the UI/UX planning of the project. I was also responsible for the project manager role, which involved overseeing the project with the help of our Scrum Master.


Agile Workflow

We used the agile method of Scrum for this project. Our sprints were 2 weeks long, and we had daily scrum meetings to keep track on what everyone was doing. At the end of the sprint we would have a Sprint Review with Elisa Oyj to give feedback on the project and Sprint Retrospective to reflect on the sprint. At the start of the new sprint, we would have a Sprint Planning meeting where we would decide on the tasks for the next sprint with the help of the Elisa personnel.


Personal Retrospective

Personally I enjoyed and learned a lot from this project. I was able to delve deep into the software development process and understand the agile methods that we used. I also deepened my understanding on Angular Framework and to my personal surprise I also learned how to create a responsive roadmap -styled yearly calendar with filtering based on project type and time.
